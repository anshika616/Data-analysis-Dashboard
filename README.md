# Mobile-Sales-Data-Analysis(Interactive dashboard using powerbi)
## Project Objective
This analyzes mobile phone sales data to provide insights into overall business performance. It highlights key metrics such as total sales, total quantity sold, total transactions, and average selling price, along with interactive filters for month, brand, mobile model, and payment method.

# Questions (KPIs)
-What is the total revenue, quantity sold, number of transactions, and average selling price of mobile phones?
-How do mobile sales vary month by month, and which months show peak or low demand?
-Which days of the week generate the highest and lowest mobile sales?
-Which cities and regions contribute the most to total sales?
-Which brands generate the highest sales and number of transactions?
-What are the top-performing mobile models based on total sales?
-How does customer purchasing behavior differ by payment method (UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Cash)?
-Which payment method is most preferred by customers?
-What is the overall customer satisfaction level based on ratings (Good, Average, Poor)?
-How do sales and transactions change when filtering by brand, model, payment method, or month?
